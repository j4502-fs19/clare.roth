About

My name is Clare Roth and I am a journalism student at the University of Misssouri. Although I am interested in a career in financial reporting, I am also interested in street photography, cartoons and history.

I am fluent in German and proficient in Turkish. In high school, I lived in Vienna, Austria, for a year as a foreign exchange student. Later, I traveled to Baku, Azerbaijan, where I learned Turkish through a U.S. Department of State Critical Language Scholarship.
